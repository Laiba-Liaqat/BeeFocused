# BeeFocused
BeeFocused: The Distraction-Aware Focus Ecosystem
BeeFocused is a minimal, distraction-aware productivity app where:
🐝 Bees hover and collect nectar when you stay focused.
🥀 The hive goes dormant if you switch apps or leave the session.
🍯 Your focus history builds a golden, thriving apiary.
📊 Your productivity is analyzed with honey-yield charts and hive analytics.

Instead of tracking boring streaks, the app visualizes your productivity as a living, humming ecosystem that evolves the more you focus.

✨ Core Features
⏳ Focus Timer
Flexible Presets: 15m, 25m, 45m presets and custom duration options.

Live Interaction: Real-time countdown with Pause and Give Up options.

Dynamic Animation: Custom-painted bees dynamically circle and hover around the hive entrance during active sessions.

🐝 Gamified Focus System
Nectar Collection: Bees actively gather resources while you maintain your focus.

Distraction Shield: If you switch apps or leave the session, the bees retreat and the "honey" is lost—creating a real consequence for distraction.

Progressive Visuals: Watch the hive activity evolve from quiet buzzing to high-energy collection as your session duration grows.

🌳 3D Meadow Visualization
Immersive Environment: Your completed sessions populate an interactive 3D meadow.

Depth-Field Rendering: Placed hives are sorted by Y-coordinates, scaling dynamically to create an authentic 3D depth illusion.

Living Ecosystem: As your productivity increases, your meadow transforms into a bustling, golden landscape of thriving hives.

📊 The Apiary Dashboard & Analytics
The Apiary screen provides a comprehensive dashboard to help you understand your focus patterns:

Yield Statistics: Total Nectar Collected, Abandoned Sessions, Total Focus Time, and Average Session Duration.

Peak Activity Chart: Identify when your "swarm" is most productive (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, or Night).

Productivity Analytics: Toggle between Day, Week, Month, and Year views to track your growth over time.

Honeycomb Calendar: A grid-based focus calendar that highlights your most consistent days.

🌗 Theming
Dynamic Modes: Elegant Light and Dark modes.

Golden Aesthetic: A clean, nature-inspired palette featuring warm golds, rich ambers, and deep meadow greens.

👛 Solana Web3 Integration
Phantom Wallet Simulation: Fully integrated Web3 screens for wallet connections.

On-Chain Incentives: Built for a Web3-native experience, featuring simulated Solana devnet minting procedures and interactive token claim ledgers for your "harvested" focus.

🧠 The Concept
Most productivity apps track time. BeeFocused tracks consequences.

Stay in the app: The bees work, nectar is gathered, and your honey reserves grow.

Leave the app: The bees abandon the hive, and your progress is reset.
